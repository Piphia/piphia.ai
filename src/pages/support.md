# Contact &amp; Support

Piphia is **local-first** and experimental — happy to help you get the most out of
it.

## Get help

- **Documentation** — start with the [Quick Start](/docs/quick-start) and the
  [Troubleshooting](/docs/troubleshooting) guide; most questions are answered there.
- **Issues &amp; bug reports** — open an issue on our
  **[GitHub repository](https://github.com/Piphia/piphia.ai)**. Please include your
  OS, App version, and steps to reproduce.
- **Questions &amp; ideas** — use **GitHub Discussions** on the same repository.

## Email

For anything that doesn't fit a public issue, reach us at
**[contact@piphia.ai](mailto:contact@piphia.ai)**.

## Before you write

- The App runs entirely on your machine — we **cannot** see your data, notes, or
  configuration, so please describe the problem and steps to reproduce.
- AI features are **experimental** and depend on the model/provider _you_
  configure; if an agent misbehaves, note which model/provider you used.
